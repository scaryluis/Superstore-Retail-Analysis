# Retail Sales Performance & Optimization Analysis

## Overview
This project analyzes retail sales data to identify profitability drivers, discount impact, and regional underperformance using Excel and Tableau. The analysis focuses on uncovering inefficiencies in pricing strategy and product performance to support data-driven business decisions.

---

## Objective
- Identify which product categories drive revenue vs. profit  
- Evaluate the impact of discounting on profitability  
- Analyze regional performance to locate underperforming areas  
- Provide actionable recommendations to improve overall profitability  

---

## Tools & Skills
- Microsoft Excel (Pivot Tables, Data Analysis)
- Tableau (Dashboards & Storytelling)
- Data Visualization & Business Analysis
- Profitability & Margin Analysis
- Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

---

## Dataset
The dataset includes:

- Retail transaction data across multiple U.S. regions  
- Product categories and sub-categories  
- Sales, profit, quantity, and discount data  
- Customer segments and geographic information  

---

## Key Insights

- **Furniture generates the highest total sales but has a very low average profit margin (~3%)**, indicating poor profitability efficiency  
- **Technology is the most profitable category**, consistently generating strong margins despite lower total sales than Furniture  
- **Discounting significantly impacts profitability**, with margins turning negative at higher discount levels (typically above ~20%)  
- **The Central region is the only loss-making region**, with an overall average profit margin of approximately **-10.4%**  
- **Other regions (West, East, South) maintain positive margins ranging from ~16% to ~22%**, highlighting a clear regional performance gap  
- **Losses are concentrated in Furniture within the Central region**, making it the primary driver of overall underperformance  

---

## Business Recommendations

- Reduce discount levels above ~20% to prevent margin erosion  
- Reevaluate pricing strategy for Furniture to improve profitability  
- Investigate operational and market inefficiencies in the Central region  
- Focus on higher-margin products and optimize product mix  

---

## Project Deliverables

- Excel-based data analysis using pivot tables and profitability calculations  
- Interactive Tableau dashboard for executive-level overview  
- Tableau Story presenting a structured, step-by-step business analysis  
- Key insights and actionable business recommendations  

---

## Outcome

This project demonstrates how data analysis and visualization can be used to identify profitability issues, diagnose root causes, and recommend strategic actions. The findings highlight the importance of pricing strategy and regional performance in driving overall business success.

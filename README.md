# Retail Sales Performance & Optimization Analysis

## Overview
Analyzed retail sales data to identify profitability drivers, discount impact, and regional underperformance using Excel and Tableau.

---

## Objective
- Identify revenue vs. profit drivers by product category  
- Evaluate the impact of discounting on margins  
- Analyze regional performance  
- Provide data-driven recommendations  

---

## Tools
- Excel (Pivot Tables, Analysis)  
- Tableau (Dashboard & Story)  

---

## Key Insights
- Furniture drives the highest sales but has a low profit margin (~3%)  
- Technology is the most profitable category  
- Discounts above ~20% lead to negative profitability  
- Central region is the only loss-making region (~-10.4% margin)  
- Other regions maintain positive margins (~16%–22%)  

---

## Recommendations
- Reduce discount levels above ~20%  
- Reevaluate pricing strategy for Furniture  
- Investigate Central region performance  
- Focus on higher-margin products
